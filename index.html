Help/FAQ

1. How to locate nearby hospitals?

Use our real-time map to locate hospitals near your current location. The service uses GPS to track the closest hospitals available 24/7.

2. How to contact ambulance services?

Call the number displayed on our ambulance service page or use the emergency contact button for immediate assistance. You can also book an ambulance via our app for real-time tracking.

3. What should I do in case of a medical emergency?

Always contact emergency services immediately. Use our app to find nearby hospitals and notify the ambulance service with your location for quick assistance.

4. How can I check hospital availability?

Click on the hospital name on the map to see current bed availability, ER status, and estimated wait times for emergency services.
