Welcome to Ambu Flow

Ambu Flow is a revolutionary service designed to streamline the process of tracking and dispatching ambulances during emergencies. Our platform integrates real-time location tracking, route optimization, and seamless communication to ensure faster response times in critical situations.

About Ambu Flow

Ambu Flow offers:

  • Real-time ambulance tracking
  • Automated route optimization for faster arrivals
  • Communication interface between hospitals, patients, and ambulance staff
  • Emergency notifications and alerts
  • Seamless integration with hospital ER systems

Key Features

Ambu Flow's platform is designed with features that enhance emergency medical services:

  • Real-time Tracking: Monitor ambulance locations at all times.
  • Smart Routing: Optimized routes based on traffic and distance.
  • Patient Management: Real-time communication with hospital emergency departments.
  • Emergency Alerts: Get instant alerts for emergency situations.
